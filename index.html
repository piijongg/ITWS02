About Chigiri

HYOMA CHIGIRI (千ち切ぎり 豹ひょう馬ま Chigiri Hyōma?) is a contender of Blue Lock, who currently plays as a left wing for England's Manshine City during the Neo Egoist League.

Chigiri's Characteristics

  1. Chigiri is shown to have great focus being a player who is constantly running through tight spaces, between players, and even switching from dribbling to passing.
  2. He strives to leave an effect on the field, which causes other players to notice him and acknowledge his abilities.
  3. Most Aggressive Player on the Pitch
